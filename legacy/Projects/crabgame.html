Finding Mother is a "crabillicious" crab puzzle platformer, with simple drag and shoot controls and a unique stacking mechanic. Adventure as a king crab through an underwater and city zone with unique enemies, friendly crabs, and deadly challenges. Will you ascend and find the mother crab, or perish along the way?
Finding Mother was developed in unity by 4 people with no prior Unity or Pixel art experience. There is a lot of jank still left but we are all super proud of the game and plan the continue to work on it and add more content. If you encounter a bug or want to send us a suggestion please let us know by DMing us on discord (Mega567835, Nebulanet, Aj, and Wild_Mortimer).
